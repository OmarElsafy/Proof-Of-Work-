About Me

I specialize in translating complex blockchain technologies into clear, compelling narratives that drive adoption and understanding. With deep expertise in DeFi protocols, infrastructure, and emerging crypto primitives, I've helped leading web3 projects communicate their value propositions to diverse audiences.

My work spans long-form research reports, ecosystem content strategy, social media campaigns, and educational video content. I've contributed to projects like Avalanche, Wormhole, Quai Network, Space and Time, and currently focus on decentralized science with DeSciWorld.

From analyzing CLOB wars and appchain innovations to exploring stablecoin adoption and DeSci infrastructure, I believe in the power of web3 to reshape how we coordinate, transact, and create value.
